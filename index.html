About ENSTA

The National Higher School of Advanced Technologies (ENSTA) is one of the premier institutions specialized in the fields of science, technology, mathematics, and computer science. Born from the merger of the National Higher School of Technology and the Higher School of Applied Sciences, ENSTA inherited their property, rights, obligations, personnel, and resources, establishing itself as a leading center for advanced technological education and research excellence.
